What is Ànúrà?

Ànúrà is a social impact initiative founded to support and empower young girls and women as they explore opportunities within science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics.

We create accessible opportunities, creative challenges, and educational resources that help girls build confidence and discover their potential in STEAM. At Ànúrà, we believe talent and curiosity exist everywhere, and that every girl deserves the opportunity to explore the skills, ideas, and careers that shape the future.

We are committed to reducing fear, intimidation, and exclusion from STEAM spaces by creating intentional entry points that feel accessible, creative, and affirming.

Our Journey So Far

Since founding in 2023, Ànúrà has reached 20+ girls through direct programs, and established partnerships with organizations committed to education access.

Community

20+ Girls Reached - Through STEM hackathons, virtual programs, and community engagement, we've directly impacted girls across Nigeria and beyond.

First STEM Hackathon - In August 2024, 18 girls participated in our flagship event in Lagos with keynote speaker Ms. Lola Aworanti-Ekugo (Chief Digital Officer at FBNQuest).

Partners

Strategic Partnerships - Collaborating with Slum2School and The Bloom Africa to ensure access for girls from underserved communities.
