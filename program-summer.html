🌟 August Summer Workshop

Intensive in-person and virtual programs designed to immerse girls in STEM learning

About Our Summer Programs

Ànúrà's Summer Workshop is an intensive, immersive experience designed to give girls deep exposure to STEM concepts, real-world problem-solving, and hands-on innovation. We've run both in-person hackathons and virtual learning programs to make participation accessible to girls across Africa.

🎯 2024: STEM Hackathon (In-Person)

Event Details

Date & Time: August 29, 2024 | 9:30 AM - 2:00 PM

Location: Slum2School, Lagos, Nigeria

Theme: Collaborative problem-solving through technology and innovation

What Happened: 18 girls came together to form teams, engage in guided STEM challenges, and develop technological solutions to real-world problems. Girls experienced what it's like to work in innovation labs, collaborate with teammates, and present ideas to an audience. Winning team "Team Ruby" was award a ₦50,000 cash prize.

Key Features

  • Keynote from Ms. Lola Aworanti-Ekugo (Chief Digital Officer, FBNQuest) on careers in technology
  • Hands-on STEM challenges and collaborative team projects
  • Certificates of Participation for all attendees
  • Networking opportunity with peers and mentors

Program Structure

Format: Interactive virtual workshops and collaborative projects

Frequency: Three sessions per week

Platform: Google Classroom + WhatsApp for resource sharing and community connection

What's Covered: Digital design, animation, STEM fundamentals, and technology innovation. Girls worked on creative projects, participated in interactive exercises, and built confidence presenting their work.

Key Features

  • Guest speaker session with Ntýam Princia on personal growth and STEM opportunities
  • Interactive workshops exploring multiple STEAM disciplines
  • Hands-on projects with real-world applications
  • Collaborative learning environment and peer support
  • Certificate of Participation and final showcase presentation

What You'll Experience

  • Guided Workshops - Expert facilitators walk you through STEM concepts and creative techniques step-by-step
  • Project Collaboration - Work in teams to solve real problems, simulating what real innovation labs and tech companies do
  • Mentorship Opportunities - Connect with women working in STEM fields who can share their experiences and advice
  • Network with Peers - Meet girls from across Africa who share your passion for STEAM and build lasting friendships
  • Hands-On Creation - Build, design, code, or create—not just learn theory, but apply it

Who Should Apply?

Our Summer Workshop is for girls aged 10-20 who are curious about STEAM and ready to dive deep. No prior experience necessary—just bring your curiosity and willingness to learn.
