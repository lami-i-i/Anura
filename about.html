About Ànúrà

Learn about our mission to empower young girls and women in STEAM.

What We Do

Ànúrà is a social impact initiative founded to support and empower young girls and women as they explore opportunities within science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics.

We create accessible pathways for girls to learn, experiment, and discover their potential in STEAM spaces. We believe that talent and curiosity exist everywhere, and that every girl deserves the opportunity to explore the skills, ideas, and careers that shape the future.

Our Core Pillars

📚

Education

Providing beginner-friendly resources and learning opportunities that make STEAM accessible to all girls.

🎨

Creativity

Encouraging creative exploration and innovation through challenges, projects, and activities.

🤝

Community

Building supportive networks where girls can connect, share ideas, and inspire one another.

Our Mission

Empower young girls and women in STEAM by creating access to learning opportunities, creative exploration, and supportive communities that build confidence and encourage innovation.

Our Vision

We envision a future where girls and women feel confident exploring STEAM spaces, sharing their ideas, building creative solutions, and contributing to the future of innovation.
